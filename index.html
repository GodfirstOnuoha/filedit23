About FILEDIT'23

Fostering Innovations and Leveraging Advanced Disruptive Technologies 2023

FILEDIT'23 is the theme for 2023 NACOS Southeast Zonal Convention and it is the largest gathering of NACOSites, Developers, Tech Enthusiast, Designers, Entrepreneurs, Experts, Computer Professionals and Students in the Southeastern part of Nigeria.

At FILEDIT'23, we will explore the art and science of innovation, showcasing real-world examples, and inspiring you to unlock your creative potential. During this convention, we will delve deep into the world of disruptive technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, IoT, and more. We will explore how these technologies are reshaping traditional paradigms and creating new opportunities.

FILEDIT'23 is not just a convention; it's a journey into the future. We will feature keynote speakers who are visionaries and pioneers in their respective fields. Workshops and panel discussions will provide a hands-on understanding of how innovation and disruptive technologies can be applied to solve real-world challenges. You'll have the opportunity to network with like-minded individuals, startups, and industry leaders who are at the forefront of these exciting developments.
